Places from which CloudCoin can be purchased

You can purchase from independent sellers at their price. Some sources have automated systems that allow you to
download your coins immediately after purchase. This is the easiest and fastest way to buy CloudCoins, though
the convenience will result in a slightly higher price.

Buy CloudCoins

CloudCoins can be purchased directly from any individual seller by any agreed upon currency, trade or barter exchange,
completely off the record and without being linked to any ledger or receipt.

CCE (CloudCoin Ethereum)
on the Cryptocurrency Exchanges

CloudCoin Ethereum (CCE) is an Ethereum contract with a 1:1 tie to CloudCoin. This allows you to buy CCE on a crypto
exchange and swap them for CloudCoins, or to liquidate your CloudCoins by trading your CloudCoins for CCE to to sell on
crypto exchanges. This method, while it can be associated with slightly lower coin costs, is time consuming and requires
transaction fees.
